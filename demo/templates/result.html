sponsoredBye: skip those pesky sponsor sections

With the increase of sponsored YouTubers AND the non-skipable advertisements in between videos, we lose a lot of time, when watching YouTube. Unfortunately we cannot solve the problem of the unskippable advertisements (unless you purchase YouTube Premium or use Adblocker). What we CAN however do is skip the skippable sponsored-by segments inside of videos. That is what sponsoredBye does: give you an indication on when sponsored segments start and stop, so that you do not have to listen to these sections again and can watch your videos in piece. sponsoredBye works in 3 steps

  1. We retrieve the video transcript
  2. We use a State-of-the-art Transformer model to predict the sponsor timestamps
  3. We give you these time stamps back so you can skip them
